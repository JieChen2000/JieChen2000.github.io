Here's a tool to calculate after tournament rating change.

Usage:
1. Click the "Add New Match Row" button to add a new row to the table if you played more than 1 match in the tournament
2. Enter oppenent rating and select wether you win the match for each match you had in the tournament
3. Click the "Calculate My New Rating" button to get your newly calculated rating.

Disclaimer: the calculation is based on USATT rating explaination, assuming your opponents' ratings are not adjusted.
